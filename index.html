About Golden Plate

Where Excellence Meets Ethiopian Warmth

Located in the heart of Bole at the Abssinia Building on Cameroon Street, Golden Plate Restaurant has established itself as a premier western dining destination in Addis Ababa. With a commitment to fresh ingredients and skilled preparation, we offer a diverse menu that blends classic favorites with contemporary creations.

Our stylish and welcoming atmosphere makes us the perfect choice for family gatherings, celebrations, and casual meals alike. Whether you're joining us for lunch or dinner, our attentive staff ensures every guest feels valued and cared for.
